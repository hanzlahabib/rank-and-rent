| # | Domain | Why |

| 2 | `hendersonevinstall.com` | Short, memorable, keyword-rich |
| 3 | `evchargerhenderson.com` | Service-first naming |
| 4 | `hendersonevpros.com` | Trust signal in name |
| 5 | `evinstallhenderson.com` | Simple, direct |
| 6 | `hendersonchargerpros.com` | Professional feel |

**Naming rules:**
- Include city name (Henderson) — critical for local SEO
- Include service keyword (ev, charger, install)
- .com only — most trusted for local businesses
- Keep under 25 characters
- No hyphens

---

## Content Strategy

### On-Page SEO Rules:
1. **Title**: `[Service] in Henderson, NV | [Business Name]` (under 60 chars)
2. **Meta Description**: Unique per page, include CTA + phone number mention (under 155 chars)
3. **H1**: One per page, primary keyword + location
4. **H2s**: Secondary keywords as subheadings
5. **Body**: 800-1500 words per service page, 1200-2000 words per blog post
6. **Images**: Alt tags with keyword + location
7. **Internal links**: Every page links to 2-3 related pages

### Blog Post Schedule:
Publish all 8 posts at launch, then 1-2/month ongoing.
